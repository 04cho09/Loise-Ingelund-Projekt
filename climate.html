The Climate

The climate with today's emission of greenhouse gases is more unpredictable, as the ocean is getting hotter and the ocean has become more acidic because of it. The increased levels of atmospheric carbon dioxide over the past few decades could threaten the biodiversity and productivity of ocean ecosystems due to the higher acidity affecting the balance of minerals in the water, and that could make it difficult for certain marine animals to build their protective skeletons or shells.

Not such a fun fact, a further 15-25cm of sea level is expected to rise by 2050.
