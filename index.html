The ocean and the life within

The ocean is a beautiful place, filled with creatures that swim among the waves. As the planet is covered in almost 80% water you would assume we would be curious as to what type of creature inhabits it, but only 5% of the ocean is explored and charted. The reason we don't explore our oceans is because the immense pressure in the deep ocean that makes it extremely difficult to explore, since that environment makes it extremely dangerous for us.
